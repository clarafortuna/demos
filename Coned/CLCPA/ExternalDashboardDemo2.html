Electric Vehicle Charging
Expanding Access to Clean Transportation

Transportation is responsible for 26% of New York City's greenhouse gas emissions, with elevated exposure in communities near highways and bus depots. In 2024, Con Edison doubled EV charger installations in disadvantaged communities (DAC), advancing equitable access to clean transportation.
